# 實體動物卡寫實動作圖片 Prototype Prompts

> 產生方式：Codex built-in Imagegen
> 日期：2026-07-18
> 用途：測試 122×175mm 動物卡在黑白雷射列印下的寫實動作圖片

## 共通方向

- 寫實自然史動作圖，不是肖像或大頭照
- 動物全身與特殊能力動作完整可見
- 橫向 3:2，主體約占畫面 70–75%，保留裁切空間
- 淺色極簡背景、高明度、可控灰階、清楚外輪廓
- 無卡通、無著色本線稿、無血腥、無文字與水印

## 非洲象：鼻子噴水

```text
Use case: photorealistic-natural
Asset type: black-and-white laser-printed children's animal game card artwork
Primary request: Create a highly realistic natural-history action image of a full-body African elephant performing its special ability: raising its trunk high and spraying a clear arc of water.
Subject: anatomically accurate adult African elephant; large fan-shaped ears, long curved tusks, wrinkled skin; all four legs and the complete trunk visible; the face and eyes remain recognizable.
Style/medium: photorealistic natural-history illustration with real wildlife anatomy and skin texture; not a portrait.
Composition/framing: dynamic 3:2 landscape composition, three-quarter side view, entire animal visible, subject fills about 75 percent of the frame, generous crop-safe margin, water arc stays inside the frame.
Scene/backdrop: minimal pale natural ground and very light neutral background; no dense habitat.
Lighting/mood: high-key daylight, crisp outer silhouette, clear separation between legs, trunk, tusks, and water spray; controlled grayscale midtones suitable for a monochrome laser printer.
Constraints: child-friendly; water spray must remain visible when converted to grayscale; no text, no border, no watermark.
Avoid: headshot, portrait crop, cartoon, coloring-book line art, dark background, large solid-black areas, blood, injured animals, extra animals, clutter.
```
