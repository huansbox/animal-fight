## 寶物 Prompt

### 萬用背包

```text
Create a clean grayscale children's board-game card illustration of an upright adventure backpack, front three-quarter view, on a pure white background. The backpack is open wide and clearly contains a coiled rope, one short wooden plank, a small wrench, and three standard six-sided dice peeking out. Make the backpack the unmistakable single main object, occupying about 70 percent of the canvas. Use a semi-realistic educational picture-book style for children age 5–9: accurate familiar object shapes, friendly but not cute or cartoony, five-value grayscale only, medium-bold clean black contours, broad simple shading, sparse interior detail, high contrast suitable for black-and-white laser printing. Add a faint grounding shadow only. No scenery, no people, no animals, no labels, no letters, no numerals, no logo, no watermark. Landscape 3:2 composition with generous white margins.
```

### 探險手電筒

```text
Create a clean grayscale children's board-game card illustration of a familiar handheld adventure flashlight placed diagonally from lower left toward upper right, on a pure white background. Keep the flashlight as the main recognizable object and the rock fragment secondary, together occupying about 70 percent of the canvas. Use a semi-realistic educational picture-book style for children age 5–9: accurate familiar object shapes, friendly but not cute or cartoony, five-value grayscale only, medium-bold clean black contours, broad simple shading, sparse detail, high contrast suitable for black-and-white laser printing. No full scene, no people, no animals, no labels, no letters, no numerals, no logo, no watermark. Landscape 3:2 composition with generous white margins.
```

### 變化魔鏡

```text
Create a clean grayscale children's board-game card illustration of an upright oval magic mirror with a simple sturdy frame, on a pure white background. Outside the mirror at lower left sits one standard six-sided die showing a clearly low face with one pip; inside the mirror reflection the corresponding die clearly shows four pips, communicating transformation from failure to success. At the top crown of the mirror frame, place three small identical dice, all showing the same face, as a special trigger motif. Make the mirror the unmistakable single main object, occupying about 70 percent of the canvas. Use a semi-realistic educational picture-book style for children age 5–9: accurate familiar object shapes, slightly magical but not ornate, friendly but not cute or cartoony, five-value grayscale only, medium-bold clean black contours, broad simple shading, sparse interior detail, high contrast suitable for black-and-white laser printing. Add a faint grounding shadow only. No scenery, no people, no animals, no labels, no letters, no numerals, no logo, no watermark. Landscape 3:2 composition with generous white margins.
```

## 後援與三區 Prompt

### 全隊後援

```text
Using the referenced capybara, meerkat, and toucan only as the visual style and recurring-character reference, create a new 3:2 landscape grayscale illustration for a children's cooperative board-game reserve-help card. Show the capybara at left already bracing a simple fallen branch but needing help; from the right, the meerkat clearly arrives to take over while carrying the loose end of one short rope; the toucan flies just above them pointing toward the open path. The action must instantly read as “another teammate arrives and takes over,” not as danger or combat. Keep all animals anatomically believable and mature, with the same simplified natural-history V2 print style: exactly five discrete grayscale values including paper white, medium-bold clean contours, broad body planes, sparse texture, high contrast for black-and-white laser printing. Minimal ground line and one branch only, no full scene. Every full body, wing, foot, tail, beak and rope stays inside frame, with generous white margins. No cute cartoon exaggeration, no clothing, no text, no symbols, no border, no logo, no watermark.
```
