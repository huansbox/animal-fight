# 暴雨森林救援隊揭露卡場景 Prompt

> 用途：170×118.5mm 黑白雷射揭露卡的情境插圖
> 風格：V2 圖鑑平衡版的場景延伸；圖片不包含文字

## 共用規格

```text
Use case: illustration-story
Asset type: 3:2 landscape scene illustration for a children's cooperative board-game reveal card, printed on a monochrome laser printer
Primary request: show one environmental problem clearly enough that a five-year-old can point to what needs to be solved. Do not show any playable hero animal and do not imply which animal or attribute is the correct answer.
Style/medium: simplified natural-history field-guide illustration matching the animal-card V2 print style; anatomically and physically believable; not photographic and not cute cartoon. Use five discrete tones including paper white, a medium print-safe outer contour, broad grayscale shapes, sparse identifying texture, and generous negative space.
Composition/framing: 3:2 landscape; one clear focal problem; complete important objects inside the frame; crop-safe margin on every edge.
Scene/backdrop: paper-white background with only the minimum forest, water, bridge, roots, or village elements needed to understand the problem.
Print construction: no smooth gradients, dense texture, stippling, crosshatching, halftone screens, hairline detail, dark full background, or large connected black mass.
Child clarity: urgent but safe; no injury, blood, horror, trapped body parts, crying close-up, threatening face, combat, text, border, logo, or watermark.
```

## 0｜任務簡報與洪水巨獸

```text
Scene: a wide forest river after heavy rain. A tall wall of rushing water mixed with a few large branches and mud rises in the middle distance, loosely suggesting a giant creature without eyes, mouth, face, limbs, or horror. Farther ahead, a small animal village shelter sits on clearly higher ground beside a visible evacuation path. Show three separate shield-like rings of water around the central flood mass as physical water arcs, not graphic icons. Keep the village, water wall, and safe high ground visually separate. No playable animals.
```

## 1｜被積水切斷的道路

```text
Scene: a forest path broken into several sections by shallow floodwater, wet mud, and two fallen logs. Show a plausible continuous route using a few exposed stones, one stable log, and a slightly raised dry patch, but do not highlight it with arrows or symbols. Water is visibly rising around the lower path edges. No animals or footprints.
```

## 2｜搖晃藤橋上的幼獸

```text
Scene: a vine suspension bridge over fast but nonviolent floodwater. The bridge head has one thick loose support vine and one drifting branch pushing against it. On a stable rock beside the bridge, one small deer fawn waits calmly with a thin vine loosely wrapped around the outside of one rear leg; no injury, panic, or tight restraint. Make the two simultaneous problems readable: stabilize the bridge and gently free the fawn. No rescue animal or implied solution.
```

Scene: one sturdy open wooden medicine crate containing six clearly separated capped glass bottles cushioned by cloth. The crate sits before a short muddy route crossed by three large slippery tree roots, leading toward a simple raised shelter in the distance. Keep every bottle intact and upright. Make fragility and careful transport understandable without broken glass, warning icons, arrows, hands, or animals.
