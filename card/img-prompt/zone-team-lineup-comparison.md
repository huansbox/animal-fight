# 三區示意隊伍標準圖比較 Prompt

> 日期：2026-07-19
> 用途：待命區／再挑戰區／休息區共用角色定案前比較
> 風格：V2 圖鑑平衡版；只比較隊伍組合，尚未演出區域狀態

> **定案**：採用 A「水豚＋狐獴＋巨嘴鳥」作為三區固定示意隊伍；B、C 保留為比較紀錄。

## 共用規格

```text
Use case: scientific-educational
Asset type: 3:2 landscape standard animal-team lineup for a children's cooperative board-game status card, printed on a monochrome laser printer
Primary request: show exactly three adult animals as one calm, capable team. This is a neutral reference lineup, not an action scene. Make all three species immediately recognizable to a five-year-old from silhouette and defining features.
Style/medium: simplified natural-history field-guide illustration matching the approved animal-card V2 print style; anatomically believable, mature and realistic, not photographic and not cute cartoon. Use exactly five discrete tones including paper white, a medium print-safe outer contour equivalent to 0.45–0.50 mm at final size, broad grayscale body planes, and sparse species-identifying texture covering only 8–15 percent of each animal.
Composition/framing: 3:2 landscape. Arrange the three animals in one shallow horizontal team lineup, all facing slightly toward the same empty direction. Keep every full body, foot, tail, beak and perch inside the frame. The team occupies 70–75 percent of the longest image dimension with at least 8 percent crop-safe white margin. Keep the smallest animal large enough to read after printing; this is a balanced team portrait, not a scientific size chart.
Scene/backdrop: paper-white background with only one minimal pale-gray grounding shape and, when required by the bird, one simple low stump or branch. No habitat scene.
Print construction: discrete flat grayscale regions only. No smooth gradients, continuous-tone lighting, photographic grain, dense fur or feather microtexture, stippling, crosshatching, engraving marks, halftone screens, hairline detail, large connected black mass, or dark background.
Child clarity: calm and alert expressions; closed mouths; no clothing, backpack, tools, weapons, prey, conflict, injury, threat, text, border, logo, or watermark.
Comparison lock: use the same framing, team scale, white space, grayscale treatment, contour weight, viewing angle and visual density for all three candidate groups. Only the animal species and the bird's necessary support may change.
```

## A｜水豚＋狐獴＋巨嘴鳥

```text
Subjects from left to right: one adult capybara standing in a relaxed three-quarter side view; one adult meerkat upright on its hind legs with forepaws relaxed near the chest; one adult toucan perched on a single low plain branch. Preserve the capybara's blunt muzzle and barrel-shaped body, the meerkat's slender upright silhouette and dark eye patches, and the toucan's oversized but anatomically plausible bill. Keep all three equally important and visually separate.
```

## 比較檢查

- 三張都必須是標準隊伍圖，不演出待命、接手或休息。
- 三隻動物縮至四分標籤卡面後仍能各自辨識。
- 沒有特定動物壓過另外兩隻，也沒有動物小到像背景裝飾。
- 黑白雷射列印不依賴細毛、細羽或連續灰階。
- 選定隊伍後，以該圖作為三區狀態插畫的角色參考。
