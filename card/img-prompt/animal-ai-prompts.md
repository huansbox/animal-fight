## 🎨 通用風格設定
*   **風格**：`black and white vector line art` (黑白向量線稿), `coloring book style` (著色本風格)
*   **線條**：`bold lines` (粗線條), `high contrast` (高對比)
*   **背景**：`white background` (純白背景), `no shading` (無陰影/無灰階)
*   **構圖**：`centered composition` (居中構圖), `full frame` (滿版)
