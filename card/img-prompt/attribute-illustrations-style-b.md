# 屬性情境插圖 - B 簡化寫實鋼筆插畫

## 共用規格

```text
Use case: scientific-educational
Asset type: final miniature board-game attribute scene, printed about 16 mm tall on a black-and-white laser printer
Input images: Image 1 is a style and character reference only. Match its simplified naturalistic pen-and-ink appearance and the same young explorer's general face, short hair, simple tunic-like shirt, trousers, and boots. Do not copy its panel frames or composition.
Style/medium: simplified realistic black pen-and-ink educational illustration; believable human proportions; mature children's science-book drawing; bold outer contour lines about 20 percent thicker than the reference; restrained medium-weight interior lines; almost no cross-hatching; not cartoon, not chibi, not photorealistic.
Composition/framing: square canvas; one single concrete action scene; tight centered crop; the action fills about 85 percent of the canvas; fully readable at 16 mm tall; keep every essential body part and object inside the canvas.
Scene/backdrop: perfectly plain white paper background.
Color palette: pure black ink and white only.
Constraints: no text, no letters, no numbers, no labels, no floating icon, no abstract symbol, no panel frame, no border, no divider, no decorative background, no gray wash, no gradient, no cast shadow, no watermark.
```

## 各屬性 Primary request

| 屬性 | Primary request |
|---|---|
| 力量 | A three-quarter view of the same young explorer lifting one very large rough boulder over their head with both hands; bent elbows and planted legs clearly show effort; the boulder and lifting action must dominate the image. |
| 速度 | A side view of the same young explorer sprinting at full speed, whole body visible in a long running stride, with only three bold horizontal motion lines behind the body. |
| 攻擊 | The same young explorer driving one clenched fist into a thick upright wooden board; the board is visibly splitting at the impact point with a few large wood fragments; show the upper body, fist, and broken board very clearly. |
| 防禦 | The same young explorer crouching safely behind one large rounded shield while three small stones strike and bounce away from the shield; the shield, protected child, and rebounding stones must all be obvious. |
| 聰明 | A waist-up view of the same young explorer studying one large unfolded route map, tracing the correct path with one finger; show the face thinking, the map, and the pointing finger clearly. |
